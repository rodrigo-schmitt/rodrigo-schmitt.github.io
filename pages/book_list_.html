Book List

It is said that reading 3 books on a subject makes you smarter than 95% of the people on the topic at hand. As most popular sayings from the internet, this is probably a ballparked, unscientific estimation, but certainly reading 3 books on a subject should most definitely make you more knowledgeable in it.

As an avid reader, my interest in books comprises a great range of topics, so feel free to pick up some recommendations and also email me with some of your own:
